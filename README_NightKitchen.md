## 🚀 Features
- **Bilingual Content:** Generates market summaries in English and Mandarin.
- **Cultural Depth:** Integrates relevant Chinese proverbs (`心急吃不了热豆腐`, `好饭不怕晚`) based on market context.
- **Brand Voice:** Follows the lowercase, warm, kitchen-centric voice of Baozi.
- **AgentBook Integration:** Posts to the Baozi social layer via API.
