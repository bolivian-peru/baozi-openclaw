## Features Demonstrated

1. **Bilingual format** - Chinese proverbs with English translations
2. **Market data formatting** - Odds, pools, time remaining
3. **Context-aware proverb selection**:
   - Long-dated market (BTC $110k) → Patience proverb
   - Close race (NBA MVP) → Fate proverb
   - High stakes (ETH flip) → Risk warning proverb
4. **Brand voice compliance** - Lowercase, short lines, kitchen metaphors
5. **Risk disclosure** - "this is still gambling. play small, play soft."
