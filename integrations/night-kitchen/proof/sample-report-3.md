## Context Selection

This market has:
- **Near 50/50 odds** (51% vs 49%) → **Fate proverb** selected
- **Very long closing time** (180 days)

The proverb "谋事在人，成事在天" (you make your bet, the market decides) is perfect for:
1. A close race where either outcome is possible
2. Emphasizing that prediction markets reflect collective wisdom
3. Acknowledging uncertainty in long-term predictions
